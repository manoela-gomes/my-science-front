"My Science" is a learning tool that makes scientific writing from NASA projects or any other scientific resources inteligible, easier and more emphatic to lay people.
It generates inclusive images and more comprehensible texts with a GPT-3 model.
It can be used for helping teachers making first grade students and functional illiterates feel included in science, for instance.
